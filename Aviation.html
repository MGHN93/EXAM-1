PT Lion Group, or Lion Air Group, is a holding company based in Indonesia. The group consists of several airlines from Southeast Asia, currently Lion Air, Wings Air, Batik Air, Batik Air Malaysia, and Thai Lion Air.

Gilang started his career as a Flight Data for the Corporate Safety division located in Balaraja, Banten.

Job desk of a Flight Data Analyst listed below:

  • Monitor flight data to identify flight hazard
  • Improve flight safety by using preventive methods : reactive, proactive, and predictive
  • Reactive method : inform crucial hazards regarding flight safety issues from gathered data
  • Proactive method : daily monitoring flight data for flight hazard assessment
  • Predictive method : flight data analysis of previous flights for future improvements of flight safety

Indonesia Aviation School

Indonesia Aviation School is the FIRST private ATS training provider in Indonesia that is certified and authorized by Indonesia's Ministry of Transportation Directorate General of Civil Aviation and PPSDM Perhubungan Udara. Our staff and instructors adhere to high standards and provide everything the student needs to succeed in the field of Air Traffic Services.

The training last for 3 months; 1 month in-class-theory & simulation, 2 month on the job training. Upon graduation, successful students are given Certificate of completion, IELP certification, and License issued by Directorate General of Civil Aviation
